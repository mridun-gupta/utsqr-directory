Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Overview

UTS QR Finder is an unofficial utility page for faster access to station QR references. We aim to keep data use minimal and transparent.

2. Data We Process

  • Local preferences saved in your browser, such as favorites and local submission history.
  • Submission details you enter in the QR contribution form.
  • Technical and usage information collected through Vercel Web Analytics, such as page views, pages visited, timestamps, approximate location based on IP address, referrer URL, and basic device and browser information. This information is sent to and processed by Vercel as a third-party analytics provider.
  • No account registration is required for basic usage.
Ad space (privacy page)

3. How Data Is Used

  • To provide app features, including search, favorites, and submission workflow.
  • To review and process QR submissions shared by users.
  • To improve quality and coverage of station QR entries.
  • To understand aggregated usage patterns, performance, and errors on the site through Vercel Web Analytics, so we can monitor, secure, and improve the service.

4. Browser Storage

This site stores some information in localStorage on your device. You can clear it anytime from browser settings, which may reset favorites and submission history.

5. Vercel Web Analytics

This site uses Vercel Web Analytics, provided by Vercel, Inc., to collect aggregated information about how visitors use the site. When you access this page, certain technical data (such as page views, pages visited, timestamps, approximate location based on IP address, referrer URL, and basic device and browser information) is sent to and processed by Vercel to provide analytics and performance insights.

Vercel processes this data on its own infrastructure as an independent third party. For more details on how Vercel handles this information, please review their privacy policy at https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

You can limit analytics collection by using browser settings or extensions that block analytics scripts or by disabling JavaScript, though doing so may affect site functionality. You can also contact us using the email below if you have questions or wish to exercise privacy rights available in your jurisdiction in relation to analytics data.

6. Third-Party Links

Links to external services (for example app stores) are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.
